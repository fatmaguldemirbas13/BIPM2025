My Academic Journey: A Geographical Map

Welcome to My Educational Path

This interactive map visualizes the key geographical locations that have shaped my academic and professional career. From my early studies in Turkey to international experiences abroad, each marker represents a significant step in my educational journey.

The journey starts in **Istanbul**, my hometown, where I completed my initial university studies. I then moved to **Ankara** for a pivotal phase of my education. Following this, I had the invaluable experience of an Erasmus semester in **Prague, Czech Republic**.

Currently, I am focused on my Master's studies, and the map highlights the location of my next major step. Click on the markers to reveal detailed information about the time period and the specific experience at each location.

Legend:

  • Blue Marker: Completed or current degree/experience.
  • Red Marker: Planned future destination (e.g., higher education).

Map generated using the Python Folium library and hosted via GitHub Pages.
